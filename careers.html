Our Vacancies

Get to know the list of perks Seobrothers gathered to make you happy.

We’re always doing our best to provide the best conditions for you, whether you’re working or just enjoying life.

Comfortable office

We rent a 500 sqm office space close to the city center. This is a modern building in close proximity to public transport, with a parking lot and canteenand. Aside from comfortable workspaces, our premises have conference rooms, a kitchen, and a common area.

Free training and further professional development

We invite industry professionals to participate in private workshops for experience exchange. Employees also get compensation for additional training, seminars, and online courses. We never stop evolving.

Free SPA and GYM for employees

Join us and enjoy exercising or simply relax in a jacuzzi after a productive day for free. All employees get access to the best SPA in the city – ESPA Riga.

Free meals from respected restaurants

We’re employing services of local restaurants to provide our office workers with an extensive menu for lunch breaks on a daily basis.

Free insurance

We provide our team members with paid sick leaves, annual vacation, and an insurance package for all their medical needs.

Great team

We’re open, we’re friendly, we’re full of life, passion, and humor. We love our work, we want people to be the most amazing versions of themselves, and we consider ourselves to be the best team in the world. The humblest, too, for that matter.
